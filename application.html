How To Play

Clear the board by matching tiles in groups of three. But thereâ€™s a catch:

  • You have six slots to hold tiles
  • Once you collect three of the same kind, they disappear
  • If your slots fill up with unmatched tiles, the game is over
  • Clearing top layers first makes hidden tiles easier to reach
  • If you grab a tile too soon, you might run out of matching options. So avoid blocking further moves
