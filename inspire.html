What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Heroes push ourselves to be the best version of ourselves that we can be.

In recent years, Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg has solidified her place as a hero to the people. A fierce advocate for women's rights, Ginsburg faced many challenges both in school and in the professional setting. Her dedication to the law led her to be appointed to the United States Supreme Court in 1993, where she served until her passing in 2020..
